About Villalobos

Villalobos is located in New Orleans, Louisiana, out in Assumption Parish. The owner is Tia Maria Torres. She is a retired Marine and has 4 children, 2 of which are twins and are also adopted. The shelter staff mainly consists of Parolees, or in other words, recently released convicts. The shelter is the home of 200+ Pitbulls and a few other breeds. Also if you are looking for a dog to adopt, please help one of the many darling creatures at the shelter. Adoption applications are made online and in-person adoptions are made by appointment. Here is the link to the Villalobos Rescue Center website if you are interested in adopting one of these sweet stinkers! But you better hurry! There are only 12 dogs left for adoption! https://vrcpitbull.com/dogs/
